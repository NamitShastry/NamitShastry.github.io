Young INTACH Newsletter Publication

In Grade 10, I achieved national recognition by winning the Best Prize Entry in a nationwide competition organized by the Indian National Trust for Art and Cultural Heritage (INTACH).

My award-winning contribution was featured in the Young INTACH Newsletter (Vol. 20 No. 4, Oct–Dec 2023), a publication that reaches young heritage enthusiasts and educators across India.

Responding to the United Nations' declaration of 2023 as the International Year of Millets, I highlighted the role of millets in food security, sustainability, climate resilience, and nutrition awareness.

This recognition reflected my ability to research and communicate solutions to nationally relevant challenges at a young academic stage.
